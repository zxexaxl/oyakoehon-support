Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

“My Voice Storybook” (the “App”) respects user privacy and strives to protect personal information.

1. Information Handled by the App

The App handles the following types of data on the user’s device:

  • Picture book page images
  • Audio recordings, including read-aloud recordings and memory recordings
  • App settings
  • Backup data created within the App

These data are created and saved by the user within the App.

The data are stored on the user’s device. The developer does not collect, access, or view the user’s picture book images or audio recordings.

2. Data Storage

Picture book images and audio recordings created in the App are generally stored on the user’s device.

They are not sent to external servers operated by the developer.

3. Backup Feature

The App allows users to export backup files (.ehonbackup) at their own discretion.

Backup files may include the following data:

  • Picture book images
  • Audio recordings
  • Book settings
  • Read-aloud settings
  • Other data created by the user

Users are responsible for managing and sharing backup files.

4. Advertising

The App uses a third-party service, Google AdMob, to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may process device information and related data for purposes such as ad delivery, ad quality, fraud prevention, and measurement.

For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. In-App Purchases

The App uses Apple’s In-App Purchase system to provide features such as removing advertisements.

Payment processing is managed by Apple. The developer does not collect or access credit card information or other payment details.

6. Camera, Microphone, and Photo Library Access

The App accesses device features for the following purposes:

  • Camera: to take photos of picture book pages
  • Photo Library: to select images
  • Microphone: to record read-aloud audio

These features are used only when the user explicitly performs the relevant actions.

7. Copyright

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the appropriate rights to use any picture books, images, audio, or other content within the App.

When using commercially published picture books or other copyrighted materials, users must comply with applicable copyright laws and the terms set by the relevant rights holders.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary.

9. Contact

For inquiries, please use the support information provided on the App Store.
